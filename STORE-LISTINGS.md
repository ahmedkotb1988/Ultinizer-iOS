# Ultinizer - Store Listings

## Apple App Store Listing

### App Name
Ultinizer

### Subtitle (30 chars max)
Household Task Manager

### Keywords (100 chars max, comma-separated)
household,tasks,chores,family,organize,todo,shared,home,collaborate,reminders

### Promotional Text (170 chars max)
Organize your household effortlessly. Create tasks, assign to family members, track progress, and keep everyone on the same page.

### Description

Ultinizer is the easiest way to manage household tasks as a family. Stop arguing about who does what — create tasks, assign them to family members, and track progress together in real time.

**Create and Organize Tasks**
Break down household work into clear, manageable tasks. Add detailed descriptions, set due dates, and organize tasks into categories like House Chores, Errands, Groceries, and more. Create subtasks to break larger jobs into smaller steps.

**Assign and Collaborate**
Assign tasks to specific household members so everyone knows their responsibilities. Add comments to tasks to communicate about details, share updates, or ask questions — all in context, right where the work happens.

**Track Progress**
See at a glance what needs to be done, what is in progress, and what has been completed. Use the dashboard for a quick overview, the task board for a visual workflow, or the calendar view to plan your week.

**Stay Notified**
Receive push notifications when tasks are assigned to you, when someone comments on your tasks, or when deadlines are approaching. Customize your notification preferences to get only what matters to you.

**View Statistics**
See household contribution statistics to understand how tasks are distributed. Track completion rates, see who is doing what, and keep things fair and balanced.

**Attach Files and Photos**
Add photos and file attachments to tasks. Snap a picture of that broken fixture, attach a receipt, or share a reference image — everything stays connected to the task.

**Dark Mode**
Full dark mode support for comfortable use day and night.

**Privacy First**
No ads. No tracking. No third-party analytics. Your household data stays private and is never shared with anyone. Delete your account and all data at any time.

**Features at a Glance:**
- Create, assign, and track household tasks
- Subtasks and checklists
- Comments and discussions on tasks
- File and photo attachments
- Push notifications
- Calendar view
- Task board (Kanban-style)
- Household statistics
- Categories and organization
- Dark mode
- Account deletion with full data removal

Ultinizer is free and built for families who want a simple, private way to manage their home together.

### App Review Notes

**Demo Account:**
- Email: husband@example.com
- Password: password123

**Notes for Reviewer:**
This is a household task management app. The demo account is pre-configured with a household called "Our Home" that includes two members (Ahmed and Sarah) and sample tasks across categories like House Chores, Errands, Work Reminders, and Groceries.

Key features to review:
1. Dashboard: Shows an overview of tasks assigned to the logged-in user.
2. Tasks: Browse, filter, and manage tasks. Tap a task to see details, subtasks, comments, and attachments.
3. Task Board: Swipe between columns (To Do, In Progress, Done) for a Kanban-style view.
4. Calendar: View tasks by due date on a calendar.
5. Statistics: View household contribution stats.
6. Settings: Manage profile, notification preferences, household settings, and account deletion.

The app requires a household to function. New users either create a household or join one via an invite code. The demo account already has a household set up.

Push notifications require a physical device and will not work in the Simulator.
